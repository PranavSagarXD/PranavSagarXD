# [Yo, I'm Pranav! 👋](https://rexd.space)

I'm a student who just loves to build stuff when I have some free time. I'm not a pro; I'm just a guy who likes to tinker with code until things work (or until they break, then I fix them). 

I'm what you’d call a **vibe coder**. For me, coding isn't about rigid rules or corporate workflows—it's about the flow, the late-night sessions, the right music, and just getting that "this looks and feels right" feeling when something finally comes to life.
